Dota 2

Dota 2 Summary

Dota 2 is a free-to-play, team-based strategy game developed by Valve Corporation, where two teams of five players compete to destroy each other’s Ancient, a key structure in their base.
It’s one of the most complex and competitive games in the MOBA
(Multiplayer Online Battle Arena) genre.

With over 120 unique heroes, deep strategic elements, and high mechanical skill requirements, Dota 2 is known for its steep learning curve and rewarding gameplay. Players choose different roles (carry, support, etc.) and work together to gain map control, defeat enemies, and secure objectives. Dota 2 is also a major esport, famous for The International (TI)—a world championship tournament with multi-million dollar prize pools, funded largely by the game's community.

It features:

  • A global competitive scene
  • Legendary players and teams
  • Constantly evolving gameplay with regular updates

Whether you're a casual gamer or an aspiring pro, Dota 2 offers a deep and endlessly replayable experience.
